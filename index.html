Andrew Morin

I am a Research Assistant Professor in the School of Cyber Studies at the University of Tulsa. My research interest is on cybercrime measurement, critical infrastructure security and digital currency econometrics. I teach Introduction to Cyber Security, Blockchain and Financial Technology Security, and Analytical Programming.

I received my PhD in Computer Science at the University of Tulsa in 2023. I received my MS in Computer Science at the University of Tulsa in 2021. I received my BS in Computer Science from the University of New Mexico in 2019 with a minor in math.
